Welcome to Encryptia City

Welcome to the fascinating world of Encryptia City! Get ready for a revolution in learning, where exciting fun merges with knowledge acquisition. In this virtual city, a multitude of adventures await you, during which you will develop your skills through engaging game-inspired tasks and missions.

This page is your guide to getting started. Here, you will learn about the tools that will accompany you on this extraordinary journey!

Encryptia City

Encryptia is the creator of the educational platform Encryptia City, which utilizes elements of computer games to teach digital skills such as programming and web design. Participants navigate a virtual city, completing various tasks, which promotes engagement and effective learning.

The platform adjusts the difficulty level to the individual skills of the user, offering personalized support and guidance through the integration of artificial intelligence technology. Additionally, participants receive support from mentors and coaches who help maintain motivation and learning structure.
